Iron Protocol

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Iron Protocol ("the App") is an AI-powered gym training companion. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to keeping your information private and secure.

1. Data We Collect

Iron Protocol collects the following types of data, all of which is stored locally on your device unless explicitly noted:

  • Profile information: Name, body weight, height, and fitness goals you voluntarily enter.
  • Training data: Workout sessions, exercise logs, sets, reps, and weights you record.
  • Nutrition data: Meals, macronutrients, and calorie logs you enter.
  • Progress photos: Photos you choose to upload for progress tracking. These are stored only on your device.
  • AI conversation history: Messages sent to the AI coach are transmitted to our API for processing. They are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the request.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized training recommendations and workout plans.
  • To display your progress over time within the App.
  • To process AI coaching requests via the Gemini API (Google).
  • To analyze uploaded photos for physique feedback when you explicitly request it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Local Storage

All personal data (profile, workouts, nutrition logs, progress photos) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (browser localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use a feature that transmits it (such as the AI coach).

4. Third-Party Services

Iron Protocol uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini API: Used to power the AI coaching feature. When you send a message to the AI Coach or request photo analysis, your message and/or image is transmitted to Google's servers. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. Standard server access logs may be collected. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Used for typography. Your browser may send a request to Google's servers to load fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of its retention. Clearing your browser or app data will permanently delete all stored information. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Iron Protocol is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Security

All communication between the App and our API is encrypted via HTTPS. Your API requests are rate-limited to prevent abuse. We do not log or store the content of AI conversations on our servers.

8. Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App at all times.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser or app data to permanently delete everything.
  • Portability: Your data is accessible in your device's local storage.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
